Members of BabyMonster:

Ahyeon, Chiquita, Pharita, Rami, Ruka, Asa and Rora

More Details Below

Members:

AHYEON

Ahyeon is a rapper and vocalist. She was the second member of BABYMONSTER revealed. She is Korean and has a brother. She passed Banana Culture Entertainment's first audition in 2017 and YG's audition in 2018. She trained for 4 years. Ahyeon speaks Korean, English and Chinese fluently. During the final evaluations, YG said that Ahyeon was the member with the greatest vocal range. Ahyeon was 16 years old when she was revealed as a member of BABYMONSTER. Jung Ah Yeon is her full name, and her Korean name is 정아현. She was born on April 11th, her zodiac sign is Aries. Her position in the group is rapper and vocalist.

PHARITA

Pharita is Thai. She was 17 years old when she was revealed as a new member of BABYMONSTER in 2023. She was a child model before debuting as a member of BABYMONSTER. Her full name is Pharita Chaikong, in Thai it is ภริตาชายคง. She was born on August 26, 2005. Her zodiac sign is Virgo and her position is vocalist in a group.

RAMI

Rami was the first BABYMONSTER member revealed. She was 15 years old at the time. Rami is Korean and for about 5 years before debuting. Her position in the group is vocalist. She started out as a children's clothing model before passing the YG audition in 2018 and becoming an idol trainee. Rami speaks English and Korean. Her full name is Shin Ha Ram, Korean name is 신하람. She was born on October 17, 2007. She is the lead singer of the group. She was a child model.

RORA

Rora is a former member of U.SSO Girl. She was part of the first generation from 2017 to 2018 alongside NewJeans' Hyein. Rora is one of the youngest members of the group. She was 14 years old when she debuted in BABYMONSTER. Her position in the group is vocalist. She was born on August 5, 2008, her full birth name is Lee Da In. Her zodiac sign is Leo. Her position in the group is vocalist.

ASA

Asa is one of BABYMONSTER's main rappers. She was 17 years old when she was revealed as a new member. Asa and Ruka are the first members of Japanese nationality to debut in a YG Entertainment girl group. She started dancing at a young age and specialized in hip-hop. Born on April 16, 2006, her full name is Enami Asa. Japanese name is 榎並杏紗. She is the group's rapper. Sign is Aries.

CHIQUITA

Chiquita is a Thai trainee who was being mentored by Lisa and has been training for over 2 years. At 14 years old, she was the youngest member (maknae) of the group at the time of their debut. Her family consists of her mother (born in 1981), her father and an older brother (born in 2006). Her full name is Riracha Phondechaphiphat, original Thai name is ริรชาพรเดชาพิพัฒน์. She is the group's maknae. Her zodiac sign is Aquarius..

RUKA

Ruka is the oldest member of BABYMONSTER. She was 21 years old when she debuted with the group. Ruka and Asa are the first Japanese members to debut in a YG Entertainment girl group. She is a former member of the Jpop group Shibu3 Project. In 2018, at the age of 16, she passed the YG Entertainment audition. During training, she was praised for her strong dancing skills, which she has mastered for 10 years. She trained for 4-5 years before her debut with BABYMONSTER. Her full name is Kawai Ruka, Japanese name is 河井瑠花. She was born on March 20, 2002, her zodiac sign is Pisces. She is a dancer and rapper in the group.
